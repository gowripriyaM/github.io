Delete Your MaxilloCare Account

If you would like to delete your MaxilloCare account and associated data, you can submit an account deletion request.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please send an email with the following information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if applicable)
  • Email subject: Account Deletion Request

Send your request to:

maxillocare.support@gmail.com

Deletion Process

  • Your request will be processed within 7 business days.
  • All personal data and uploaded medical images will be permanently deleted.
  • This process cannot be reversed.

If you need assistance, please contact our support team.
