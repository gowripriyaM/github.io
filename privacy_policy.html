MaxilloCare Privacy Policy

MaxilloCare is a dental support application designed to assist in dental fracture identification and enable communication with healthcare professionals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the MaxilloCare mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and email address when creating an account.
  • Dental images uploaded for fracture identification.
  • Medical consultation messages or related information.
  • Device information and usage data to improve application performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze uploaded dental images for fracture identification.
  • To enable communication between users and dental professionals.
  • To improve the functionality and reliability of the application.
  • To provide user support.

3. Medical Disclaimer

The information and analysis provided by MaxilloCare are intended for informational and supportive purposes only. The app does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal and medical data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Data Sharing

MaxilloCare does not sell or rent user data. Information may only be shared with healthcare professionals involved in consultation services within the app.

6. User Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

MaxilloCare does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
